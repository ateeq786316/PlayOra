Create a sophisticated, premium light theme color palette for a modern website using light green, white, and black as the foundation colors. The goal is to achieve a clean, elegant, and professional look that conveys innovation and trustworthiness.

## Color Requirements

### Primary Colors
- **Main Green**: A sophisticated, muted light green (not too bright) that feels premium and modern
- **Pure White**: Crisp white for backgrounds and negative space
- **Deep Black**: Rich black for text and high-contrast elements

### Supporting Colors
- **Secondary Color**: A complementary color that works well with the green (blue, purple, or neutral)
- **Accent Color**: A subtle highlight color for CTAs and important elements
- **Neutral Grays**: A range of grays for borders, backgrounds, and secondary text

## Design Goals
1. **Premium Feel**: Colors should evoke luxury, sophistication, and professionalism
2. **Harmony**: All colors should work together seamlessly without clashing
3. **Accessibility**: Ensure sufficient contrast ratios (WCAG AA compliant)
4. **Versatility**: The palette should work well across various UI components
5. **Modern Appeal**: Contemporary color combinations that feel fresh and innovative
